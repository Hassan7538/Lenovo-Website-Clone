Troubleshooting or Update

  1. Close all applications & restart your computer to stop background applications.
  2. Click on the Start menu and select the Settings icon.
  3. Click on Update & Security, then click on troubleshoot, present at left side of the screen.
  4. Click on additional troubleshooters
  5. Select the type of troubleshooting you want to do, then select Run the troubleshooter.
  6. Your problem will be fixed, if not ; goto Start , then goto Settings > Update & Security > Windows Update.
  7. Click Check for updates. Now, click View optional updates.
  8. Click Driver updates, then select the driver. Click Download and install.

Reinstalling Driver

  1. Right click on start and open device manager.
  2. Select category and right click on that to select properties.
  3. From there you can check that, your driver is working properly or not.
  4. Goto drivers tab. From their you can delete your driver.
  5. Goto Start , then goto Settings > Update & Security > Windows Update.
  6. Click Check for updates. Now, click View optional updates.
  7. Click Driver updates, then select the driver. Click Download and install.
